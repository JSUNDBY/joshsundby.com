About

Minneapolis-based artist and technologist

Josh Sundby creates at the intersection of technology, light, and contemplative practice. Through Digital Coherence, he designs immersive projection installations for venues and public spaces. His digital art practice explores sacred geometry, generative systems, and bold visual language.
